RBX1 is the RING-H2 catalytic subunit of every cullin-RING ubiquitin
ligase. No designed binder against it has been published. The GEM ×
Adaptyv 2026 competition collected 12,707 designs from 198 teams; 322
ran through expression and SPR at the Adaptyv Foundry. 255/322 (79\%)
expressed. 9/322 (2.8\%) bound. One reached the \emph{Strong} bin at KD
= 26 nM (\texttt{small-vole-maple}, ORBIT, Mandrake Bio). The binder
set is six miniproteins, two nanobodies, and one peptide. The
submission pool was 81 miniproteins, 33 nanobodies, 33 peptides, 10
scFvs, and 164 \emph{Other}. The full submission table, every ESMFold
prediction, and every SPR replicate are released as a public benchmark.
With 9 binders, per-method and per-modality differences are descriptive,
not significance claims.
